Development: Spring Weekend Lineup Generator

Project Background, Goals, And Applications

My development website is centered around generating Spring Weekend Lineups! Because of COVID, it is very unlikely we will get a Spring Weekend from the school. To make up for this, we can at least make our own lineups and imagine what the best weekend at Brown could have been like. The vast majority of the student population has no idea what the process is like for selecting or budgeting, so this is just a guess at what budget and selection would look like. You can select up to six artists to have in your lineup, and you will get metrics such as total cost and crowd approval based on the artists you selected. Once you have generated a lineup, you can save it so you can compare crowd approvals of the different lineups you can think up!

Usability Principles

For my usability, I made sure that interactable objects and dropdowns were clearly marked. Buttons have distinct and vibrant colors, and sorting dropdowns arrows that indicate they have more information. For Colors, I chose a dark design, with light and muted accents for readablity. I also designed this to be a one page site, that when looking at each section, can be read in order from left to right, with increasing information as you scroll down the screen. This is to adhere to natural reading and gazing tendencies of users.
